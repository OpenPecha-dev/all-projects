  94. NT — 07/20/2022

    Hi NT, Queenie, and others, It seems that the paragraph recognition model couldn't handle the large gap inside a line very well, as it's something unusual to see in non-Tibetan texts. I was provided such a possible solution and I hope you can give it a try. In Kurt's script, there is a place to specify language hints as (see also https://cloud.google.com/vision/docs/ocr#specify_the_language_optional). language_hints=["bo"] Please instead do language_hints=["bo-t-i0-handwrit"] It will use a different paragraph recognition model (designed for handwriting) that would handle this better. You may also try language_hints=["bo", "und-t-i0-handwrit"] or language_hints=["und-t-i0-handwrit"] Here is a comparison of what it should look like. Please kindly let us know how this works.
